Alternative Scans and Intellectual Property

Alternative Scans values the importance of respecting intellectual property rights. We understand that protecting original works and the rights of their creators is crucial.

Our Mission: Providing Access to Rare and Alternative Materials

Alternative Scans aims to provide access to a wide range of creative materials, including rare books, out-of-print publications, and alternative formats that may be difficult to obtain through traditional means. We believe that making these materials accessible can benefit research, education, and the preservation of cultural heritage.

Addressing Copyright Concerns

We acknowledge that some materials hosted on Alternative Scans may raise copyright concerns. We strive to operate within the boundaries of copyright law while fulfilling our mission of providing access to valuable materials.

Contacting Copyright Owners

If you are a copyright owner or authorized representative and believe that material on Alternative Scans infringes your copyright, please contact us at altscans0@gmail.com.

DMCA Takedown Notices

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been made available on Alternative Scans in a way that infringes on your copyright, you have the right to notify us under the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998.

To file a DMCA takedown notice, you must include the following information:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed:
    Provide the name of the copyrighted work, author, and any other relevant identifying information.
  • Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing or to be the subject of infringing activity and that is to be removed or access to which is to be disabled, and information reasonably sufficient to permit the service provider to locate the material:
    Provide the specific URL of the infringing material on Alternative Scans.
  • Information reasonably sufficient to permit the service provider to contact the complaining party, such as an address, telephone number, and, if available, an electronic mail address at which the complaining party may be contacted:
    Provide your full name, address, phone number, and email address.
  • A statement that the complaining party has a good faith belief that use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law:
    Include a statement that you believe in good faith that the use of the material in the manner complained of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • A statement that the information in the notification is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that the complaining party is authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed:
    Include a statement that the information in the notification is accurate and that, under penalty of perjury, you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.
  • A physical or electronic signature of the person authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed:
